## Key Advantages of Local Service

- ✅ **Predictable Pricing:** All-inclusive CAD pricing, no surprise freight or border fees
- ✅ **No Currency Risk:** Quoted and invoiced in Canadian dollars
- ✅ **Faster Response:** Tank swap or emergency service within 24–48 hours (vs. 5–7 days cross-border)
- ✅ **Lower Emergency Cost:** No 50%+ rush premiums because we're nearby
- ✅ **Simplified Logistics:** No customs, no brokerage, no buffer inventory needed
- ✅ **Hands-Off Support:** Service team nearby understands your facility and schedules
