Subject: Local high purity water support can reduce hospital service risk

Hello [Client Name],

For hospitals and healthcare facilities, water service reliability is not just a purchasing issue. It can directly affect uptime, scheduling, sterilization support, laboratory operations, and facility resilience.

A local Ontario service model can help reduce cost and operating risk by limiting:

- Cross-border freight exposure and longer logistics loops
- Customs brokerage and border-related administration
- U.S. dollar pricing volatility
- Emergency freight costs when service timing changes
- The need for larger contingency inventory to protect critical operations

Local support can also improve:

- Response time when tank exchange or service support is urgent
- Coordination with hospital schedules and access requirements
- Technical follow-up when water quality or system demand changes
- Confidence that service intervals match actual facility needs

In many cases, the savings are not only freight-related. They come from fewer disruptions, lower contingency cost, and better service continuity for critical applications.

If useful, we can review your current DI or high purity water service approach and identify where local support may improve cost control and operational reliability.
