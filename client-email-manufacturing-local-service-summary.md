Subject: Local DI service can reduce manufacturing downtime risk and cost

Hello [Client Name],

For manufacturing facilities, DI and process water service affects more than water quality. It can directly influence rinse performance, production continuity, maintenance planning, and the cost of unplanned disruption.

A local Ontario service model can help reduce total landed cost by limiting:

- Longer freight routes and added handling
- Customs brokerage and cross-border administration
- U.S. dollar pricing exposure
- Emergency shipping premiums when a tank change is urgent
- Buffer inventory costs needed to protect production schedules

Local support can also improve:

- Response time when production demand shifts unexpectedly
- Coordination with plant operating schedules and shutdown windows
- Technical troubleshooting when water quality drifts or service intervals tighten
- Confidence that service programs fit actual production conditions

In many plants, the biggest savings are not only in transport. They come from fewer delays, fewer contingency costs, and lower downtime risk.

If useful, we can review your current DI or water treatment service model and identify where local support may improve cost control, uptime, and scheduling reliability.
