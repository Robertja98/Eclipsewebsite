Case Study: DI Water Service for Eastern Ontario Hospitals

Supporting sterilization, lab prep, and equipment rinse with reliable DI tank service across multiple healthcare facilities.

Client Profile

A network of hospitals and diagnostic labs across Eastern Ontario, including facilities in Kingston, Belleville, and Peterborough. These sites require consistent high-purity water for sterilization, autoclaves, and lab instrumentation.

Challenge

Several locations were experiencing inconsistent water quality due to aging RO systems and irregular service schedules. This led to equipment scaling, rinse failures, and compliance concerns during audits.

Solution

Eclipse Water Technologies implemented a regional DI tank program with standardized service intervals and centralized tracking. Our approach included:

  • Deployment of mixed-bed DI tanks post-RO for polishing
  • Scheduled regeneration every 6 weeks, with emergency swap-out available
  • Resistivity monitoring and service logs for audit compliance
  • Dedicated support team for Eastern Ontario clients

Results

Water quality now consistently exceeds 16–18 MegOhm across all sites. Equipment performance has improved, and service disruptions have been eliminated. Facilities report smoother inspections and reduced maintenance costs.
